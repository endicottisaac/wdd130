History

Founded in 1995 by John and Sarah Doe, Rapids Adventure began as a small family business with a simple mission: to share the incredible beauty and excitement of Utah's rivers with anyone who was brave enough. What started out small has grown into a high level rafting operation.

Over last 30 years, we have upheld a high level of safety, a high standard of adventure, and exceptional customer service. Our team combines decades of river experience with modern safety knowledge, ensuring that every trip is both fun and safe. We are proud of our record for safety, our reviews of the quality of our service.
