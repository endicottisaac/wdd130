Choose Your Adventure

From peaceful family floats to heart-pounding advanced rapids, we offer unforgettable white water experiences for every skill level.

Featured Trip Experiences

Family Float Adventure

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Family Friendly

Perfect for families with children 6 and up! This gentle introduction to rafting features calm waters, stunning scenery, and plenty of opportunities to spot wildlife. Our guides share fascinating stories about the area's history and geology while ensuring everyone feels safe and comfortable on the water.

Includes: All safety equipment, snacks, and professional guide services. Life jackets sized for children available.

Classic Rapids Run

Duration: 6 hours | Difficulty: Intermediate

Our most popular adventure combines exciting rapids with spectacular canyon views. Experience the thrill of navigating moderate whitewater while enjoying a riverside lunch. This trip is ideal for adventurous beginners and experienced rafters alike, offering the perfect balance of excitement and scenic beauty.

Includes: Full day guide service, riverside lunch, all safety equipment, and transportation back to starting point.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Duration: 8 hours | Difficulty: Advanced

For experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush! Navigate through powerful advanced rapids with names like "Big Dogs Only" and "Six Seven." This demanding adventure requires participants to be in good physical condition and have previous whitewater experience. Only available during peak water conditions.

Includes: Expert guide team, wetsuit rental, premium safety equipment, energy lunch, and professional photography package.

Professional Equipment & Safety Gear

All Rapids Adventure trips include professional-grade equipment to ensure your safety and comfort on the water. We maintain and regularly inspect all gear to meet the highest safety standards.

Rafts & Watercraft

  • Self-bailing rafts for all trips
  • Oar boats for scenic floats
  • Paddle rafts for active participation
  • Paddle Boards for balance and fun

Safety Equipment

  • Coast Guard approved life jackets
  • Safety helmets
  • Throw bags and rescue equipment
  • First aid kits and emergency communication

Comfort Gear

  • Splash jackets and rain gear
  • Wetsuits
  • Waterproof dry bags for personal items
  • River shoes and booties

Additional Amenities

  • Professional photography service
  • Riverside lunch and snacks
  • Coolers with ice and drinking water
  • Transportation to/from river access points
